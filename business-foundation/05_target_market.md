# Target Market

**Primary Region:** Austria (DACH expansion later)  
**Customer Profile:** Small to medium businesses (10–100 employees)

---

## Who You're Targeting

Any SMB with repetitive, manual workflows. Common verticals:

- **Real Estate:** Lead handling, follow-up, document management
- **Trades & Services:** Job scheduling, invoicing, client follow-up
- **E-Commerce & Retail:** Order processing, inventory updates, customer communication
- **Professional Services:** Client onboarding, project tracking, reporting
- **Agencies:** Task distribution, client updates, billing

**Common denominator:** They do repetitive work manually that could be automated in 1–4 weeks.

---

## Their Problems

- **Manual data entry** across disconnected tools
- **Slow follow-up** (emails, WhatsApp, calls pile up)
- **Inefficient processes** (copy-paste, manual exports)
- **Lost context** (information scattered across emails, spreadsheets, CRM)
- **Time wasted** on admin instead of revenue-generating work

---

## Your Value Prop

**"We automate your repetitive workflows so you focus on clients & growth."**

Transform from: Manual + Slow → Automated + Scalable

**Examples:**
- "When lead comes in → auto-add to CRM → send follow-up → log in spreadsheet" (1 workflow)
- "Daily invoice export → auto-send to accounting" (1 workflow)
- "New customer → auto-create folder → send onboarding checklist" (1 workflow)

---

## How to Find Them

**Initial approach (Months 1–3):**
- Direct outreach on LinkedIn (target Geschäftsführer/CEOs)
- Local networking (Salzburg business groups, Facebook groups)
- Ask existing contacts: "Do you know a business with boring, repetitive tasks?"

**Later (Month 4+):**
- Referrals from first clients (best source)
- Google local search optimization
- Content showing specific automation wins
